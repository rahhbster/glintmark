What it does

Every word deserves a great card.

Four named themes

Folio, Immersive, Broadside, and Candlelight — each a distinct visual language for sharing what moves you.

Paste & Parse

Copy a quote from anywhere and let AI extract the text, attribution, and source automatically.

Photo backgrounds

Use your own photos or choose from curated landscape presets — mountains, coastlines, forest light.

Share anywhere

Export to Instagram, Messages, or your Photos library — sized for Stories, Feed, or any format you need.

Home screen widgets

Pin a favorite card as a widget — your reflection of the day, right on your lock screen. (Coming soon)

Smart cards

Author portraits and source-specific backgrounds — curated images that match the wisdom you're sharing. (Coming soon)
