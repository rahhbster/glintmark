How it works

Three steps from a verse
to something you'll keep.

01

Capture the words

Type a verse, paste a quote, or use AI to parse text from anywhere. Add the reference and it's yours.

02

Set the mood

Choose a theme, a gradient or your own photo, and the right typeface. Every card looks considered, never templated.

03

Keep & share

Save it to your library, set it as a wallpaper, or share a crisp square anywhere. Return to it whenever you need the light.
